Cobb Salad

Description:

This recipe will explain how to make an amazing cobb salad. I can't wait to show you the famous recipe brought down from generations in this southern home of ours!

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Fired chicken bites
  • Hard boiled eggs
  • Bacon bits
  • Veggies

Steps

  1. Add Lettuce
  2. Add veggies
  3. Add cooked chicken
  4. Add cooked bacon bits
  5. Serve to customer
